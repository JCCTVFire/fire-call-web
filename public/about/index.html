About

Contributors: Cole Daitch, Colin Mannix, Jonah Pool, Vinson Povlitz, Theodore Tasker


This site contains records of emergency calls made in Arlington County, Virginia. Users are able to view data through filters and search by a particular attribute of the call.
The data was wiped of any identifying information that could link confidential data in any regard. Zip codes were randomized throughout the county for the call metadata so exact geographical coordinates are phony.
